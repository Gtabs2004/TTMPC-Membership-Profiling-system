Our Mission

To provide excellent services to all Tubunganons.

Our Vision

A sustainable cooperative where all members enjoy an improved quality of life.

We are a community-driven cooperative dedicated to empowering our members.

Tubungan Teachers Multi-Purpose Cooperative was founded with the goal of promoting unity, sustainability, and shared growth among our members. As a multi-purpose cooperative, we provide various services that support the financial, social, and economic well-being of our members and their families. From savings and credit facilities to livelihood programs and community outreach, we are committed to uplifting lives and building stronger communities. Through trust, transparency, and teamwork, we continue to build a better future for our members and the communities we serve.

Over 30 Years of Commitment and Innovation

For more than three decades, the Tubungan Teachers Multi-Purpose Cooperative (TTMPC) has remained a pillar of support for educators and their families. What began as a small initiative rooted in the values of unity, trust, and shared purpose, has since evolved into a vibrant hub of financial services and community development. Over the years, we've grown not just in membership but also in impact—providing accessible loans, empowering savings programs, and fostering a culture of collective progress. TTMPC's journey reflects a lasting commitment to service, collaboration, and the collective advancement of every member.

A Glimpse of TTMPC's Heart for the Community

Our cooperative continues to bring its mission to life through meaningful community outreach, such as the Gift Giving Activity held at Kuyot Elementary School. Led by passionate board members and guided by the theme "We Care, We Share, We Serve," this initiative reflects our shared commitment to compassion, generosity, and service. By extending a helping hand to children in need, TTMPC not only fulfills its mission but also inspires members to live out values that extend beyond its members—reaching communities, touching lives, and inspiring collective growth.
